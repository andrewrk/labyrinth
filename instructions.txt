== How to play ==

Your goal is to get to the finish square (in red) as quickly as you can. Make
sure to check out the requirements square (in gray) along the way ;-)

== Keyboard and Mouse Controls ==

Mouse - look around
W - walk forward
S - walk backward
A - strafe left
D - strafe right
